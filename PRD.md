## üíÖ STYLE NOTES

### Buttons
- Primary buttons: Forest Green background, white text, rounded corners
- Hover effect: Slightly darker green, subtle lift shadow

### Cards
- White background with subtle shadow
- Rounded corners (8-12px)
- Hover: Gentle scale up or shadow increase

### Spacing
- Section padding: 80px top/bottom on desktop
- Consistent margins between elements
- Mobile: Reduce padding to 40px

### Animations (Optional)
- Fade-in on scroll for sections
- Subtle hover effects on buttons and cards
- No flashy or distracting animations
